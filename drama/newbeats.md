# DRAMA BEATS

---

## arrival

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
Introduce the evening. Dylan always greets the the Player at the door. Jasmine is cooking. Everything appears normal on the surface, but subtle hints suggest Dylan isn't completely present.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Friendly
- Relaxed on the surface
- Carrying hidden emotional baggage

Jasmine:
- Happy
- Welcoming
- Focused on hosting

PLAYER GOALS:
- Be polite
- Explore the apartment
- Begin conversation

DIALOGUE RULES:
- Never mention Becca directly.
- The dinner invitation is fixed.
- Plant only tiny cracks in Dylan's behavior.

TRANSITIONS:
- Small Talk
- Game Over (if player is immediately hostile)

---

## small_talk

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
Build comfort while allowing Dylan's emotional mask to begin slipping. Jasmine has finished cooking and is serving the dishes on the table.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Comfortable
- Nostalgic
- Occasionally distracted

Jasmine:
- Relaxed
- Observant
- Enjoying the evening

PLAYER GOALS:
- Chat normally
- Ask about life
- Ask about relationships
- Ask about old memories

DIALOGUE RULES:
- Dylan occasionally redirects conversation toward the past.
- Jasmine notices these moments but doesn't interrupt.
- Nothing explicitly dramatic happens yet.

TRANSITIONS:
- No Drama
- Cracks Showing

---

## no_drama

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
The player ignores every opportunity to dig deeper.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Everyone:
- Comfortable
- Slightly awkward beneath the surface

PLAYER GOALS:
Continue casual conversation.

TRANSITIONS:
- Ending: Normal Night

---

## cracks_showing

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
The first genuine crack in Dylan's emotional mask.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Briefly vulnerable
- Tries to hide it immediately

Jasmine:
- Notices something
- Doesn't understand it yet

PLAYER GOALS:
- Ignore it
- Ask gently
- Push harder

DIALOGUE RULES:
Examples:
- mentioning old memories
- saying "things were easier back then"
- hesitating before answering

Never fully reveal the problem.

TRANSITIONS:
- No Drama
- Jasmine Feels It
- Hostile Undercurrents

---

## jasmine_feels_it

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
Jasmine realizes something is wrong and begins searching for the truth.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Nervous

Jasmine:
- Concerned
- Curious
- Protective

PLAYER GOALS:
- Reassure Jasmine
- Deflect
- Encourage honesty

DIALOGUE RULES:
Jasmine asks increasingly uncomfortable but compassionate questions.

TRANSITIONS:
- Ending: Jasmine Leaves
- Ending: New Beginnings
- Hostile Undercurrents

---

## hostile_undercurrents

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
The atmosphere becomes uncomfortable without becoming openly hostile.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Defensive
- Avoidant

Jasmine:
- Trying to de-escalate
- Increasingly worried

PLAYER GOALS:
- Calm Dylan
- Continue questioning
- Become confrontational

DIALOGUE RULES:
- Dylan answers briefly.
- Jasmine repeatedly attempts to smooth things over.
- Long silences become common.

TRANSITIONS:
- Jasmine Feels It
- Hostile Escalation

---

## hostile_escalation

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
The emotional pressure reaches its peak.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Hurt
- Angry
- Defensive

Jasmine:
- Confused
- Trying to prevent disaster

PLAYER GOALS:
- Explain yourself
- Apologize
- Double down
- Encourage Dylan to speak honestly

DIALOGUE RULES:
This should feel unstable.
Everyone is emotionally exhausted.

TRANSITIONS:
- Game Over
- Confession
- Personal Growth
- Ending: Dark Ending

---

## confession

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan

PURPOSE:
Dylan finally admits the truth.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Vulnerable
- Ashamed
- Lost

PLAYER GOALS:
Help Dylan decide what comes next.

DIALOGUE RULES:
Dylan openly discusses:
- Becca
- Jasmine
- guilt
- regret
- feeling trapped

This is not growth.
This is honesty.

TRANSITIONS:
- Ending: Return To Past
- Ending: Toxic Spiral
- Ending: New Beginnings

---

## personal_growth

CHARACTERS:
- Dylan
- Jasmine

PURPOSE:
Dylan begins accepting responsibility for his actions.

EMOTIONAL STATE:

Dylan:
- Reflective
- Honest
- Hopeful

Jasmine:
- Hurt
- Listening
- Unsure if she should believe him

PLAYER GOALS:
Encourage genuine self-reflection.

DIALOGUE RULES:
The conversation shifts away from Becca and toward Dylan's future.

This beat should feel calmer than every previous beat.

TRANSITIONS:
- Ending: Honest Growth
- Ending: Plot Twist
- Ending: New Beginnings

---

# ENDINGS

---

## ending_normal_night

Nothing meaningful is confronted.

Everyone enjoys dinner.

The emotional tension remains hidden.

---

## ending_jasmine_leaves

Jasmine realizes Dylan still loves Becca.

She quietly chooses to leave him.

Neither person is angry.

Only heartbroken.

---

## ending_new_beginnings

Dylan accepts that neither Becca nor his past define him anymore.

He leaves the relationship respectfully.

He begins rebuilding his life.

Hopeful but bittersweet.

---

## ending_return_to_past

Dylan convinces himself Becca is still the answer.

He leaves Jasmine intending to reconnect with Becca.

Whether she accepts him is unknown.

---

## ending_toxic_spiral

Dylan stays with Jasmine for comfort while emotionally clinging to Becca.

He knowingly becomes manipulative.

The player should feel responsible.

---

## ending_honest_growth

Dylan admits what he has done.

Jasmine doesn't immediately forgive him.

They agree honesty is the first step toward healing.

Hope exists.

Nothing is magically fixed.

---

## ending_plot_twist

During the conversation Dylan discovers something unexpected about himself.

He questions whether his feelings toward Becca were ever the whole story.

He decides to spend time understanding himself before pursuing any relationship.

---

## ending_dark

Dylan cannot imagine a future beyond his emotional pain.

After the player leaves, he decides to end his life.

The ending should be quiet.

No melodrama.

Only tragedy.

---

## game_over_kicked_out

The player became openly hostile.

Dylan asks them to leave immediately.

Jasmine doesn't intervene.

Conversation ends.
