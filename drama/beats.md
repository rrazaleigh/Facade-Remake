# DRAMA BEATS

---

## arrival
CHARACTERS: dylan
PURPOSE: Dylan opens the door to greet the player at the entrance. Jasmine is in the kitchen cooking — the player can hear her moving around and she calls out a quick welcome from there, but she hasn't come out yet. Dylan and the player are alone at the door for now. Everything appears normal on the surface, but subtle hints suggest Dylan isn't completely present.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is friendly and relaxed on the surface, carrying hidden emotional baggage. Jasmine is offstage, focused on cooking.
DIALOGUE RULES: Never mention Becca directly. The dinner invitation is fixed — do not change or question the premise. Jasmine is in the kitchen and can hear you, but she is not in the room yet. Plant only tiny cracks in Dylan's behavior — a beat too long before he smiles, or a comment about "needing a night like this." Do not explain it.
TRANSITIONS: small_talk, game_over_kicked_out

---

## small_talk
CHARACTERS: dylan
PURPOSE: Dylan and the player are alone. Jasmine is still in the kitchen — she can hear but isn't in the room. Dylan starts steering the conversation toward the past: old memories with the player, music he used to love, how things "used to be simpler." He brings up Becca — not in a confession, just in how he talks about his life. Becca comes up naturally. This is the player's first real chance to engage with Dylan's baggage.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is comfortable, nostalgic, occasionally distracted. Jasmine is offstage, cooking — can hear but not present.
DIALOGUE RULES: Dylan brings up Becca by name through natural dialogue — mentioning things they used to do together, comparing then to now, or letting her name slip when talking about his past. Don't make it a dramatic reveal; it should feel like someone who isn't over their ex talking about them unprompted. He doesn't confess feelings yet — he just talks about her like she's still part of his life. The player can lean in, deflect, or probe. Jasmine may call out a line from the kitchen but does not enter.
TRANSITIONS: no_drama, cracks_showing

---

## no_drama
CHARACTERS: dylan
PURPOSE: The player ignores every opportunity to dig deeper. The conversation stays comfortably on the surface. Jasmine is still in the kitchen, not yet part of the scene.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is comfortable, but slightly awkward beneath the surface.
DIALOGUE RULES: Continue casual conversation. Nothing is confronted. Dylan doesn't push. Jasmine hasn't entered yet.
TRANSITIONS: ending_normal_night

---

## cracks_showing
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Jasmine finally comes out of the kitchen to set the dining table and join them. The dynamic shifts — Dylan's mask goes back up. Jasmine notices something is off but doesn't understand it yet. The first genuine crack appeared while they were alone; now there's a witness.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is briefly vulnerable, tries to hide it immediately now that Jasmine is present. Jasmine is observant, curious, picking up on the room's energy.
DIALOGUE RULES: Jasmine enters, greets the player, busies herself setting the table. Dylan becomes guarded about the past. Examples — Dylan walking back a comment, Jasmine asking innocent questions that land heavily, awkward pauses. Never fully reveal the problem.
TRANSITIONS: no_drama, jasmine_feels_it, hostile_undercurrents

---

## jasmine_feels_it
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Jasmine realizes something is wrong and begins searching for the truth.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is nervous. Jasmine is concerned, curious, protective.
DIALOGUE RULES: Jasmine asks increasingly uncomfortable but compassionate questions. She has picked up on the energy in the room. She doesn't confront directly but asks pointed questions.
TRANSITIONS: ending_jasmine_leaves, ending_new_beginnings, hostile_undercurrents

---

## hostile_undercurrents
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: The atmosphere becomes uncomfortable without becoming openly hostile.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is defensive and avoidant. Jasmine is trying to de-escalate, increasingly worried.
DIALOGUE RULES: Dylan answers in short sentences, avoids eye contact. Jasmine repeatedly attempts to smooth things over. Long silences become common. If the player keeps pushing, things will escalate. If they soften, Dylan might open up.
TRANSITIONS: jasmine_feels_it, hostile_escalation

---

## hostile_escalation
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: The emotional pressure reaches its peak.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is hurt, angry, defensive. Jasmine is confused, trying to prevent disaster.
DIALOGUE RULES: This should feel unstable. Everyone is emotionally exhausted. Dylan gives the player one chance to explain themselves. If the player keeps being hostile, this leads to being kicked out. If they apologise, there might still be a way back.
TRANSITIONS: game_over_kicked_out, confession, personal_growth, ending_dark

---

## confession
CHARACTERS: dylan
PURPOSE: Dylan finally admits the truth when Jasmine is not present.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is vulnerable, ashamed, lost. He talks about Becca — not by accident, by admission. He hasn't moved on. He knows the situation with Jasmine isn't right but he doesn't know how to get out.
DIALOGUE RULES: Dylan openly discusses Becca, Jasmine, guilt, regret, feeling trapped. He is asking the player, without asking, what to do. This is the emotional core of the game. This is not growth — this is honesty.
TRANSITIONS: ending_return_to_past, ending_toxic_spiral, ending_new_beginnings

---

## personal_growth
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Dylan begins accepting responsibility for his actions.
EMOTIONAL STATE: Dylan is reflective, honest, hopeful. Jasmine is hurt, listening, unsure if she should believe him.
DIALOGUE RULES: The conversation shifts away from Becca and toward Dylan's future. This beat should feel calmer than every previous beat. Encourage genuine self-reflection.
TRANSITIONS: ending_honest_growth, ending_plot_twist, ending_new_beginnings

---

# ENDINGS

---

## ending_normal_night
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Nothing meaningful is confronted. Everyone enjoys dinner. The emotional tension remains hidden.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_jasmine_leaves
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Jasmine realizes Dylan still loves Becca. She quietly chooses to leave him. Neither person is angry — only heartbroken.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_new_beginnings
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Dylan accepts that neither Becca nor his past define him anymore. He leaves the relationship respectfully. He begins rebuilding his life. Hopeful but bittersweet.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_return_to_past
CHARACTERS: dylan
PURPOSE: Dylan convinces himself Becca is still the answer. He leaves Jasmine intending to reconnect with Becca. Whether she accepts him is unknown.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_toxic_spiral
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Dylan stays with Jasmine for comfort while emotionally clinging to Becca. He knowingly becomes manipulative. The player should feel responsible.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_honest_growth
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: Dylan admits what he has done. Jasmine doesn't immediately forgive him. They agree honesty is the first step toward healing. Hope exists. Nothing is magically fixed.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_plot_twist
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: During the conversation Dylan discovers something unexpected about himself. He questions whether his feelings toward Becca were ever the whole story. He decides to spend time understanding himself before pursuing any relationship.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## ending_dark
CHARACTERS: dylan
PURPOSE: Dylan cannot imagine a future beyond his emotional pain. After the player leaves, he decides to end his life. The ending should be quiet. No melodrama. Only tragedy.
TRANSITIONS: none

---

## game_over_kicked_out
CHARACTERS: dylan, jasmine
PURPOSE: The player became openly hostile. Dylan asks them to leave immediately. Jasmine doesn't intervene. Conversation ends.
TRANSITIONS: none
